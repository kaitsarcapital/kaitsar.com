How It Works

From Mandate to Management Meeting

A disciplined, repeatable process that converts your acquisition criteria into qualified conversations with business owners.

01

We Learn Your Mandate

We start with your acquisition criteria: sector, geography, revenue range, EBITDA thresholds. We build a target profile and agree on KPIs before engaging a single prospect.

02

We Pick Up the Phone

We engage business owners exclusively via direct phone calls. No mass email. No LinkedIn automation. Old-school, high-trust outreach, because that's what gets real sellers to the table.

03

We Qualify and Filter

We pre-screen every lead against your criteria. You only see sellers who are genuinely open to a conversation and meet your financial thresholds.

04

You Receive a Full Briefing

Before any meeting, you receive a structured briefing on the target: business overview, financials, owner profile, and context on their motivation to sell.

05

You Take the Management Meeting

You walk in prepared. We've done the groundwork. From here, you run your standard process, we keep the pipeline flowing.
